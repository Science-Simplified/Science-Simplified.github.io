About this Site

Hi! I'm J.J., and this website is my passion project in which I will summarize groundbreaking scientific research and its potential impact on subjects I find interesting. Content will be published weekly.

I have a few things I will be working on to improve this site over time. Some things on my list are:
1. RSS feed: I will be working on creating an RSS feed for this site where each release will be sent to your RSS reader.
2. Donation page: There will be a page for you to help support this project. There will be perks likely including #3.
3. Email newsletter: I will create a newsletter for you to receive this project over if you do not want to receive it over RSS. This will likely be a paid feature, as email newsletters are not free, and may cost around $10 per year, with that including other perks.
